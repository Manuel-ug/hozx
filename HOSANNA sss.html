The history of the school

Hosanna Secondary School was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide affordable and quality education to students in the local community and beyond. The school began with just 50 students and 5 dedicated teachers, operating from a modest facility with limited resources. Despite the humble beginnings, Hosanna Secondary School quickly gained a reputation for academic excellence, strong moral values, and holistic development.

Driven by its motto, "Empowering Minds, Building Futures," the school expanded its academic programs and infrastructure over the years. By 2010, new classrooms, a science laboratory, a library, and a computer lab were constructed to support an increasing student population and growing curriculum needs.

Located in the heart of Adjumani 1km Away from main Road, Hosanna secondary school offers condisive enrimenment for the learners Adjumani teachers for learning.
